Critique

The top infographic1 did a good job in deliever the key statement: The Median age of the first marriage only decreasedin three states from perood 2006-2010 to period 2015-2019. The left visualization has high information density. We can see both the change in median age and the poulation density of each state. It has distinguished the states that has medianage decreased with contrasting colors blue and red, which make audience noticed the3 states immediaiely. However, the two visualizations on the right has low relative low information density. Espetially the lower plot on;y gives the infotmation that DC population is nearly the half of the whole population. Also the top the 4 statistical data on the top can be difficult to comprehend with visualizatoin.

Improvements

Firstly, we can increase the functionality by combining the lower right visualization with the top right visualization , and make it a stacked bar chart by the 4 geographical segment of USA (West, East, North, South). Then we can removed the 4 statistical data and make it a error bar chart of the two periods to represent the absolute values instead of the changes like the left vis, which can increase the dimensionality and reduced the abstractness and reduncdency.

Infographic 2

Visualization wheel

Critique

The top infographic2 did a great job giving high information density visualization with figurations. We can see that states above and below the median is clearly distinguished with contrasting color blue and orange. The line chart on the right is innovative and figurative to represent the change in median age state-wise. Also, the change trend, the change extent, and the states is visually and functionally represented, whcih allow us to clearly spoted the informaiton time-wise and geographical-wise. However, to fit in the space the lower right visualization is shrinked and is hard to read of the detailinformation of the USA states maps.

Improvements

The lower right visualization can be improved by focusing on the main USA states land, and zoom in on the Alaska , so that both regions can be viewed clearly, and the space can be used more effitiently. Also, it is also good to give average median line in the scatter plot to so that audience can spoted the distance between the average median values age and the median age of the state. If the three edge dot can be labeled with states, the textual explaination can be saved, so that increase the figuration and reduce the audience distraction from text.
