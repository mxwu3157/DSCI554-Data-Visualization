2. Resolving afterimages

💡 How to resolve the colors in a negative afterimage
To find which colors are seen in a negative afterimage you use the additive color model. The colors seen are simply the complementary colors. This is because fixating lessens the response of the associated photoreceptors and we end-up seeing a mix of the colors detected by the two other photoreceptors. This effect is usually prevented by micro-saccades.
